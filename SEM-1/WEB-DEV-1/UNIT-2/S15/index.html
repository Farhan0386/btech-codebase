Lecture 15: The Box Model

Every element on a webpage is a box. We control its size using 4 layers.

1. The 4 Layers (Analogy)

Inspect this blue box to see the layers in DevTools!

I AM THE CONTENT (The Gift)
The space around me is Padding (Bubble Wrap).
The line around that is Border (The Box).
The space outside the blue line is Margin (Space between gifts).
I am a neighbor element. Notice the gap (Margin) between us?

2. Margin Examples

A. Uniform (margin: 20px) - Equal space all around.

I have 20px margin on all sides.

B. Individual Sides - Small top margin, HUGE bottom margin.

I have 50px margin on the bottom!
(I am pushed down by the box above)

C. Shorthand (Clockwise Rule)

margin: 10px 50px 20px 5px;
Top: 10px
Right: 50px
Bottom: 20px
Left: 5px

D. Centering (The "Magic" Code)

margin: 0 auto;
I am centered horizontally!
(Requires a width)
